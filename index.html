Ch Amir Kamboh

Politician

I am Ch Amir Kamboh, originally from Village Rachand in the Sheikhpura district of Punjab, Pakistan. With a Bachelor of Arts in Education, I have dedicated my career to politics and serving my community. Currently, I reside in Dubai, where I continue my political work and strive to make a positive impact. Thank you for visiting my website to learn more about my journey and initiatives.

Interests: Community Development, Education Advocacy, Cultural Exchange, Social Welfare, Public Policy

Background

My family has deep roots in the region, with our forefathers originally hailing from Punjab, India. During the partition in 1947, they migrated to Sheikhpura, Pakistan. Our family's primary occupation has always been farming, a tradition we have upheld to this day. The Kamboh caste, to which I belong, is known for its agricultural expertise and historical significance in the region. Our clan, Thind, is one of the prominent clans within the Kamboh community, known for its resilience and dedication to farming and community service.
